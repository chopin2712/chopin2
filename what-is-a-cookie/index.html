Today let's go into the world of cookies! These are great tools to spy, advertising and also remembering your passwords, etc.

What is a cookie

A cookie is a little text document that is stored on your computer. It contains a name, a value a domain and an expiration date.

A cookie can only work on the same domain: for example if a cookie comes from Facebook, Google cannot read it.

Your passwords are not directly stored using cookies but creates an ID generated by the website such as "s%3AwbxYM8zxZRrkBr8czbpVI_jkHyFSrfBF.3n32H4OPM7%2BauWGS9S17gkWbNa2bjlEFP9IyLwTw8To"

So, how Google can get information about you even when you are browsing a totally different website?

Good question! When a website decides to add ads they also add a transparent image containing a cookie. In fact, an image on a website is just like a window to another website, so the cookie is not coming from the website's domain but from Google or other ad company.

Hack the cookies : )

Now let's hack these cookies, you can automatically block ads and tracking cookies (also called "tier-party cookies") you can use Brave.

But this is not what we will do now, this quick tutorial works on Brave, Chrome and all other browsers who work on Chromium. It also works on Firefox but this is not the same thing.

  1. Go on the website where you want to get the cookies
  2. Right click and choose "inspect" you can also press CTRL+SHIFT+I
  3. Go into the "Application" tab
  4. Click on "Cookies" and choose the new item in the submenu
  5. Now you got all of your cookies, notice that if you click on the value twice you can change this value. For example on Ecosia the number of trees planted are located into a cookie called "ECFG". So if you locate the number of trees, for me it is into "t=1660" t means trees and 1600 is the number of trees.
  6. Reload the page to see the effects.

I used this technique to get back the number of planted trees from another browser.

NB: Remember that if you use Ecosia you need to make your shields down to make sure that the ads are effective

Bonus - Install Ecosia

If you don't want to install a new extension but you want to use Ecosia then just follow these simple steps:

  1. Go into settings
  2. Go into the "search engine" tab
  3. Click on "Manage search engines" then click on add

4. Click on add
5. Right click and choose "Set as default"

Now you can use Ecosia in the URL bar.

Conclusion

So now you know how to hack your cookies, notice that the cookie-hack is also a technique used by hackers to take the control of some accounts. I hope you will test this technique and use Ecosia every day! (no link with the topic but Ecosia is cool).

Never stop learning and have a nice day!
