Be anonymous online

To be anonymous online Snowden used Tor.  Tor Browser is a browser that allows you to remove your IP address.  Of course, it is nothing if you use an account.  To be anonymous, you must completely remove the use of apps that require an account.  We will see later how to find alternatives.

Tor operates thanks to three intermediaries who are classic users of Tor.

Guard: The guard is the only one to know who you are, but he does not know your request and do not know where to go.  He passes this one to the Bridge

Bridge: He does not know anything and passes it to the exit

Exit: He knows where you want to go, but he does not know who you are if you do not use an account.

Thanks to this system your identity is protected.

If you do not want to use something as radical as Tor I suggest Brave.  With Brave all advertising cookies are blocked automatically, even if we can disable the blocker.

It also has a system that allows other Brave users who make content on the internet to be remunerated.

It also has a super-private mode that can be activated with CTRL + ALT +  N, this mode uses Tor and DuckDuckGo to hide the requests to not send  them to Google.
