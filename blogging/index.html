You want to start a blog like this one? But you don't know what platform to choose, I recommend you to choose Ghost it has been built for blogging not creating websites.

Wordpress vs Ghost

Wordpress is not the same purpose as Ghost, Ghost has been built FOR blogging Wordpress SUPPORTS blogging. If you want a full easy-to-make business website I recommend you to use Wordpress but if you just want to blog and having few pages then go on Ghost!

Many people would say that Medium is made for that too, but Ghost is open source, completly free, hackable and can support other intgrations than Medium. Also you are completly free from a depending authority with Ghost because you host your website yourself!

How to install ghost UNDER TEST ENV

Recently the Ghost v3 version has been released and I upgraded my website to this v3 very quickly. First you needs to install Ghost-cli tool.

sudo npm install -g ghost-cli

If you got errors like me you just needs to restart the command, at the second time it should works.

Then you just needs to create a new instance: 

mkdir ghost
cd ghost
ghost install local

Then you will get an output in green saying that you can visit your website at localhost:2368.

You just needs to follow each steps one by one.

Configure your blog

Once you are in your blog you will needs to cofigure few things

  1. Go into Tags and create some new categories for your websites
  2. Go into Design and add links to the major categories you created
  3. Go into Pages and create some new pages like the about page to explain what is the purpose of your blog and who you are.
  4. You can change the Theme if you want
  5. You can add integrations like comments or other things.

Write a blog post

This is completly subjective but this is my method to write blog posts:

  1. Do something cool in your topic and takes pictures
  2. Get a catchy title
  3. Introduce your subject to make people want to see the rest of the post
  4. Write a structure with the titles
  5. Write your body text
  6. Add your pictures into your body text
  7. Add a conclusion who will summary the post and give some links to go further.
  8. Get tags and add a slug
  9. Review your post
  10. Publish it.

PS: Make sure to not write something too long less than 800 words is good. Except if you add code like me into your post who are counted into the word count.

Make your blog available world wide

To make it available world wide, I recommend you to convert it into static website it will be easier to public, faster and more secure. I made a post about that!

Conclusion

So now you know why you should choose Ghost instead of WordPress or Medium as blogging platform. I highly recommends you to check this out: https://ghost.org/vs/wordpress/.

You can also check other of my posts too, I will upload a post on how to publish things on the dark net for example, so stay tuned!
