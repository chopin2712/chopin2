The king of all browsers - Brave

  • SnowCode

10 Nov 2019 • 3 min read

You don't like Chrome but your appreciate how fast it is. But you want more privacy, you appreciate Tor for its privacy but you want fast browsing. Then welcome to Brave.

Brave is an open source browser based on Chromium so it gets as fast as Chrome, but even more because it also has a tracker-blocker, ad-blocker and tier-party cookies blocker. So it is even faster.

Fast, ad-free, freedom

Brave is fast because of Chromium and of the tracker-blocker. So all ads from Google are blocked and the cookies who goes with too. It create a more clear page and a faster browsing because the page is fresh and free from any trackers who goes with.

Tor integration

You want even more private mode, then you will love the super-private mode. I've already talked about it before in my other posts, you can access to it by pressing ALT+SHIFT+N. You access into a browsing mode with DuckDuckGo (no tracking with that) and Tor (even less tracking).

If you activate this mode and do not use accounts or downloading malwares or adwares then you got a really private experience because with all of this no data is stored on your computer so nobody knows what you visited.

Extensions

Some don't want to go on Brave because they want the Chrome extensions. They don't get a point. Brave is based on Chromium, Chromium can install the Chrome extensions, so Brave can too. They are really well integrated in Brave.

I use everyday the Chrome extensions: Dark Reader, Sprint Reader, MetaMask, Onion Browser Button (go on Tor all the time), Privacy Pass (skipping the cloudflares captchas), Enhancer for Youtube.

So if you are stopped from Brave for the extensions, comes, you can get most of the extensions of internet!

Open source

Brave is open source, the goal of Brave is to get back the power to you as user from your digital activities. You can get more informations about Brave on the GitHub Repo: https://github.com/brave/brave-browser

Brave is avaiable for all platforms as Windows, OSX, Linux (yeah, love it), Android and IOS. But the mobile versions don't benefit from all features like Tor browsing for example. But they're good too!

Basic Attention Token (BAT)

Finally the basic attention token is a way to remmunerate the other users of Brave. You can buy them or getting them by attention that you have to Brave ads (one per, day, hour, you decide) you can also disable this features if you want a 100% ad free experience.

Even if I never saw one single add from Brave or anything else I got tokens. Don't know from where they comes I acltually gots 4.4 BAT. Note that these have a deadline, to make sure you spend them for other creators and not acumulate them. I love this concept too!

You can also activate the auto-contribute but I don't know too much about it.

Conclusion

I highly recommends to switch on Brave now! You can get all informations on thier official website: https://brave.com/ please tell me in the comments bellow what you think about this amazing software!

I am completly in love with this browser! It is what I love with internet.

Hope you enjoyed this post.
