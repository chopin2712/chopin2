Today I wanted to talk you about a book that comes up this year that I read (binge reading actually). It's the auto-biography of Ed Snowden called "Permanent Record". This book, that I found in French (I am belgian) is called "Mémoire Vive" in French and what's wrote under it is "The man who risked his life to disclose the global mass surveillance".

Nothing will ever be removed

Summary

In 2013 a 29 man called Edward Snowden, surprise the world by dislosing that the USA had the project to collect all of our phone calls, text, emails and more to build a global mass surveillance program, never made on earth. Who could be able to look deep in the privacy of everybody on earth. Six years later Snowden reveal for the first time how he participated to the building of this programm and how he disclosed it to the world.

"Permanent Record" is the amazing story of a smart child who grown up "online", a man who became a spy, a whistleblower, and the awareness of the internet.

It will become a classic of our times.

What is it about?

The Permanent Record explains all the life of Ed Snowden, from his childhood to where he is today. It describe also how the CIA and NSA works so if you like that you will have also background details.

I will not describe this book in detail so please buy it!

Bonus

When the book came up in the USA the governement wanted to claim all the rewards from the book because of the old actions of Snowden.

Conclusion

I don't wanted to describe all the book so if you are interrested into open source, mass surveillance, Tor, anonymous on internet, and Snowden itself.

I am interrested in all of this, in fact my nickname is a contraction of Snowden and Code.

If you want to get this book you can get it on Amazon or in library (go in a library to discover other nice books).
