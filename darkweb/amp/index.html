What is dark web

There is actually many dark webs. Many peoples have confusion with the term dark web and deep web. The deep web correspond to anything stored on the internet but not visible with Google.

For example, your mailbox, if you search your email address on another computer you will not be able to check your emails without having login. Then congratulation! You're on the deep web!

But the dark web is another thing. There is many dark web and they needs a specific software to run and to be watched. These are usually anonymous and where also made to represent the golden age of internet.

The Golden Age of internet represents the time before people should consider they virtual life like they real life; when capitalism didn't took internet; when NSA was not watching the internet to find terrorists.

This correspond to the time before this dark time of the 11 September 2001.

Dark web?

First you need to say: "which one" There is many dark webs. Freenet, Tor, Dat, IPFS, ZeroNet, etc.

The most oppular darknet is probably Tor. Against many other darknets tor is NOT decentralized. Tor is masking your indentity using a special proxy called "Tor Proxy" (very original, yeah).

If you want to understand how Tor works you can check my other post on how to be anonymous on internet and how.

What can you find on the dark web?

In the darknet you can found many things that represents the human being. So you can find illegals things but also very good things.

Private emssengers for peoples who are under high surveillance like China for example.

Platform to communicate sensitives infos to the journalists via SecureDrop, in fact SecureDrop has  been devlopped by Edward Snowden.

A platform devloped by Tor themselves to send data like WeTransfer, but under the Tor network.

You also have blogs on the darknet, peoples are interrested to run blogs under the darknet because it can target a very specific audience, and also because the .onion extension is free. Same things for other decentralized platforms.
